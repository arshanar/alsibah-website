HVAC Installation, Testing & Commissioning Services

(UAE & GCC Market Compliant)

Reliable HVAC installation, testing, and commissioning services delivered in compliance with UAE authorities and international standards, designed for optimal performance in extreme Gulf climate conditions.

We provide comprehensive HVAC installation, testing, and commissioning (T&C) services across residential, commercial, and industrial projects in the UAE and Gulf region. Our services are delivered in full compliance with local authority regulations, international standards, and manufacturer guidelines, ensuring reliable and energy-efficient performance under extreme Gulf climatic conditions.

Compliance & Standards

  • Dubai Municipality (DM), Trakhees, DCD, ADM, Estidama requirements
  • ASHRAE, SMACNA, and CIBSE standards
  • LEED and Estidama Pearl Rating guidelines (where applicable)
  • Approved shop drawings, method statements, and project specifications

Scope of HVAC Services

Split Air Conditioning Units

Installation and commissioning of split and packaged units for villas, offices, and retail spaces.

  • Indoor and outdoor unit installation
  • Refrigerant piping with insulation as per UAE codes
  • Electrical power and isolator connections
  • Vacuuming, gas charging, and performance testing
  • Final commissioning and client demonstration

Ducted Air Conditioning Systems

Complete ducted HVAC solutions designed for high-efficiency cooling.

  • GI / PI duct fabrication and installation (SMACNA compliant)
  • Thermal and acoustic insulation
  • Supply, return, and fresh air systems
  • Air balancing and static pressure testing
  • Thermostat and control integration

VRF / VRV Systems

Turnkey VRF/VRV solutions for commercial and mixed-use developments.

  • Outdoor and indoor unit installation
  • Nitrogen pressure testing and leak detection
  • Control wiring and centralized controller setup
  • System addressing and optimization
  • Manufacturer-compliant commissioning

Chiller Systems

Installation and commissioning of air-cooled and water-cooled chillers.

  • Chiller placement and vibration isolation
  • Chilled water and condenser piping
  • Pumps, valves, and expansion tanks
  • System flushing and chemical cleaning
  • Electrical and control system integration

Air Handling Units (AHUs)

Professional AHU installation for centralized HVAC systems.

  • AHU installation and alignment
  • Duct and chilled water connections
  • BMS and control panel integration
  • Filter, damper, and actuator testing
  • Airflow measurement and validation

Central Cooling Solutions

Integrated central cooling systems for large-scale projects.

  • Chillers, AHUs, FCUs, and pump integration
  • Energy-efficient system configuration
  • High reliability under extreme temperatures
  • Provision for future expansion
  • Authority approval readiness

Testing & Commissioning (T&C)

  • Pressure testing and refrigerant leak detection
  • Electrical load, safety, and interlock checks
  • Air and water balancing
  • Functional performance testing
  • BMS integration and control calibration
  • Submission of commissioning reports

Handover & Documentation

  • Authority-ready commissioning reports
  • Operation & Maintenance (O&M) manuals
  • As-built drawings
  • Warranty and compliance certificates

Project Applications

Residential villas, high-rise buildings, commercial offices, retail developments, hotels, industrial facilities, warehouses, healthcare and institutional projects.
