Electrical Installation & Low-Voltage Systems

UAE & GCC Market Compliant Solutions

End-to-end electrical wiring, cabling, lighting systems, panel board installation, and low-voltage solutions delivered in compliance with UAE utility authorities and international electrical standards.

We provide comprehensive electrical installation services for residential, commercial, and industrial projects across the UAE and Gulf region. Our works are executed by qualified electricians and engineers, ensuring safety, reliability, and authority approval readiness.

Compliance & Standards

  • DEWA, ADDC, SEWA, FEWA and Kahramaa regulations
  • Dubai Municipality (DM) and Civil Defense (DCD) requirements
  • IEC, BS, and IEEE standards
  • Approved shop drawings, method statements, and materials

Scope of Electrical Services

Electrical Wiring & Cabling

  • Conduit, trunking, and cable tray installation
  • Power, lighting, and equipment wiring
  • Armored and non-armored cable laying
  • Earthing and bonding systems
  • Insulation resistance and continuity testing

Lighting Systems Installation

  • Indoor and outdoor lighting systems
  • LED and energy-efficient lighting solutions
  • Emergency and exit lighting
  • Lighting controls and sensors
  • Lux level testing and adjustment

Panel Boards & Power Distribution

  • Main Distribution Boards (MDB)
  • Sub-Main & Final Distribution Boards (SMDB / FDB)
  • Load balancing and circuit identification
  • Protection devices and labeling
  • Testing and commissioning

Low-Voltage (ELV) Systems

  • Structured cabling (data & voice)
  • CCTV and access control systems
  • Fire alarm containment and wiring
  • Public Address (PA) systems
  • System testing and integration

Testing & Commissioning

  • Continuity and insulation resistance testing
  • Earth resistance measurement
  • Functional testing of power and lighting systems
  • Panel load verification and phase balancing
  • Authority inspection readiness

Handover & Documentation

  • Authority-compliant test reports
  • As-built drawings
  • Operation & Maintenance (O&M) manuals
  • Warranty and compliance certificates

Project Applications

Residential villas, apartment buildings, commercial offices, retail spaces, hotels, industrial facilities, warehouses, healthcare and institutional projects.
