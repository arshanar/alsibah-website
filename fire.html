Fire Fighting & Fire Alarm Systems

UAE Civil Defense & GCC Compliant Solutions

Design-coordinated installation, testing, and commissioning of fire fighting and fire alarm systems in strict compliance with UAE Civil Defense regulations and international fire safety standards.

We provide complete fire protection solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects across the UAE and Gulf region. Our services are executed by trained technicians and engineers, ensuring life safety, asset protection, and authority approval readiness.

Compliance & Standards

  • UAE Civil Defense (DCD / ADCD) requirements
  • NFPA, BS, and EN fire safety standards
  • Local authority and consultant-approved specifications
  • Approved shop drawings and material submittals

Scope of Fire Protection Services

Fire Fighting Systems

  • Fire hose reel and landing valve systems
  • Automatic sprinkler systems
  • Fire pumps (main, jockey, diesel)
  • Fire water tanks and piping networks
  • Pressure testing and system flushing

Fire Alarm Systems

  • Addressable and conventional fire alarm systems
  • Smoke, heat, and beam detectors
  • Manual call points and sounders
  • Control panels and annunciators
  • System programming and cause & effect

Specialized Fire Protection

  • FM-200 and clean agent systems
  • Kitchen hood fire suppression systems
  • Gas detection and emergency shutdown systems
  • Integration with BMS and ELV systems
  • Compliance testing and certification

Testing, Commissioning & Approvals

  • Hydrostatic and flow testing
  • Fire pump performance testing
  • Fire alarm functional and loop testing
  • Authority inspection coordination
  • Final Civil Defense approval support

Handover & Documentation

  • Civil Defense–approved test reports
  • As-built drawings and layouts
  • Operation & Maintenance (O&M) manuals
  • Warranty certificates and compliance approvals

Project Applications

Residential buildings, commercial offices, retail and mall developments, hotels, warehouses, industrial facilities, hospitals, and institutional projects.
