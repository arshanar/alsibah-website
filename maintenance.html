Maintenance Services

HVAC & MEP Preventive & Corrective Maintenance – UAE & GCC

Reliable and cost-effective maintenance solutions to ensure uninterrupted performance, safety, and longevity of building MEP systems.

We provide comprehensive preventive, corrective, and annual maintenance contract (AMC) services for MEP systems across residential, commercial, and industrial facilities. Our maintenance programs are tailored to meet UAE authority requirements and manufacturer recommendations.

Maintenance Approach

  • Preventive maintenance to reduce breakdowns
  • Corrective maintenance and emergency response
  • Planned maintenance schedules and reporting
  • Compliance with safety and authority regulations

Scope of Maintenance Services

HVAC Maintenance

  • Chillers, AHUs, FCUs and VRF systems
  • Filter cleaning and replacement
  • Gas charging and leak detection
  • Performance testing and efficiency optimization

Electrical Maintenance

  • MDB, SMDB and DB inspection
  • Lighting systems and controls
  • Earthing and insulation resistance testing
  • Load balancing and fault rectification

Plumbing & Drainage Maintenance

  • Water supply and sanitary systems
  • Pumps, tanks and booster systems
  • Leak detection and rectification
  • Drain cleaning and blockage removal

Fire Fighting & Alarm Maintenance

  • Fire pumps, sprinklers and hose reels
  • Fire alarm panel and device testing
  • Routine inspections as per Civil Defense
  • Annual certification and reporting

ELV & Low Voltage Systems

  • CCTV, access control and intercoms
  • Structured cabling and network testing
  • PA, SMATV and BMS support
  • System troubleshooting and upgrades

Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

  • Customized AMC plans (monthly / quarterly / yearly)
  • 24/7 emergency support (as per contract)
  • Dedicated maintenance team
  • Detailed inspection reports and recommendations

Facilities We Serve

Residential buildings, villas, commercial offices, retail outlets, hotels, hospitals, warehouses, factories, and mixed-use developments across the UAE and GCC.
