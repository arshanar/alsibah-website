Plumbing & Drainage Systems

UAE & GCC Market Compliant Solutions

Complete water supply, sanitary, stormwater, and sewage solutions delivered for residential and commercial projects in compliance with UAE authority requirements and international plumbing standards.

We provide end-to-end plumbing and drainage services for villas, high-rise buildings, commercial developments, and industrial facilities across the UAE and Gulf region. Our systems are designed for durability, hygiene, and efficient water management under regional environmental conditions.

Compliance & Standards

  • Dubai Municipality (DM), ADM, Trakhees and local authority regulations
  • UPC, BS, and IPC plumbing standards
  • Dubai Civil Defense (DCD) coordination requirements
  • Approved shop drawings, materials, and method statements

Scope of Plumbing Services

Water Supply Systems

  • Cold and hot water distribution systems
  • PPR, PEX, HDPE, copper, and GI piping
  • Water tanks, pumps, and booster systems
  • Pressure testing and flushing
  • Fixture connections and commissioning

Sanitary & Drainage Systems

  • Soil, waste, and vent piping systems
  • uPVC, HDPE, and cast iron piping
  • Floor drains, traps, and inspection chambers
  • Leak testing and flow verification
  • System commissioning

Stormwater Drainage

  • Roof and surface drainage systems
  • Rainwater downpipes and channels
  • Catch basins and manholes
  • Flood prevention and slope verification
  • Authority inspection readiness

Sewage & Wastewater Systems

  • Sewer piping and manhole installation
  • Connection to municipality sewer networks
  • Sewage lifting stations and pumps
  • Leak and pressure testing
  • Final commissioning and handover

Testing & Commissioning

  • Hydrostatic and leakage testing
  • Water pressure and flow testing
  • Drainage slope and flow verification
  • Functional testing of pumps and accessories
  • Authority inspection coordination

Handover & Documentation

  • Authority-approved test reports
  • As-built drawings
  • Operation & Maintenance (O&M) manuals
  • Warranty and compliance certificates

Project Applications

Residential villas, apartment buildings, commercial offices, retail developments, hotels, industrial facilities, warehouses, healthcare and institutional projects.
