Mechanical Works

UAE & GCC Market Compliant Solutions

Comprehensive mechanical works delivering efficient, compliant, and reliable building services systems for residential, commercial, and industrial projects across the UAE and Gulf region.

Our mechanical works cover the full lifecycle of HVAC and related systems, from installation through testing, commissioning, and final handover. All works are executed in accordance with UAE authority regulations, international standards, and approved project specifications.

Compliance & Standards

  • Dubai Municipality (DM), Abu Dhabi ADM, Trakhees requirements
  • ASHRAE, SMACNA, CIBSE standards
  • Estidama and LEED guidelines (where applicable)
  • Consultant-approved drawings and method statements

Scope of Mechanical Works

HVAC Systems

  • Split, ducted and packaged air conditioning systems
  • VRF / VRV systems
  • Chillers, AHUs, FCUs and cooling towers
  • Chilled water and condenser water piping
  • Thermal and acoustic insulation

Ventilation Systems

  • Fresh air and exhaust air systems
  • Basement and car park ventilation
  • Kitchen exhaust and make-up air systems
  • Smoke extraction systems
  • Air balancing and performance testing

Pressurization & Smoke Control

  • Staircase and lobby pressurization systems
  • Smoke management systems
  • Control integration with fire alarm systems
  • Testing as per Civil Defense requirements

Testing, Commissioning & Handover

  • Pressure testing and leak detection
  • Air and water balancing
  • Functional performance testing
  • BMS integration and calibration
  • As-built drawings and O&M manuals

Project Applications

Residential villas and towers, commercial offices, retail developments, hotels, hospitals, industrial facilities, warehouses, and infrastructure projects across the UAE and GCC.
